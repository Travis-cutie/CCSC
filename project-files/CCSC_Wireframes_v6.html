Part 01 — Brand History
Origin
From the Mountains of Taiwan
Chi Cha San Chen was founded in Taipei in 2018, building on over 25 years of tea-making heritage cultivated by our parent group since 1998. Our belief: that tea is more than a drink — it's a ritual, a pause, a connection to something timeless.
Photo — Taipei Flagship
Photo — Tea Farm
"Every cup tells a story — of soil, season, and the hands that brought it to life."
— Founder, Chi Cha San Chen
Timeline
1998
Tea Heritage Begins
Our parent group establishes its tea-making roots in Taiwan — sourcing, crafting, and refining with precision from day one.
2018
Chi Cha San Chen Founded
The brand launches in Taipei, built on 20 years of accumulated tea expertise. The first store opens in Da'an District.
2019
First US Location — Los Angeles
Beverly Hills flagship opens, introducing America to premium Taiwanese tea culture.
2024
500+ Locations Worldwide
Present in 14 countries, maintaining the same craftsmanship as day one.
Part 02 — Our Teapresso Machine
Quality Control
The Teapresso Machine
At the heart of every Chi Cha San Chen cup is our patented Teapresso Machine — a proprietary tea-extraction system developed in-house that brews each serving fresh, at precise temperature and pressure, ensuring a consistent cup every single time.
Photo — Teapresso Machine
Fresh-Brewed Every Time
No pre-brewed concentrate. Each cup is extracted to order, preserving the full aroma and flavour profile of the tea leaf.
Precision Temperature Control
Optimal extraction temperature is calibrated per tea variety — protecting delicate oolongs from bitterness while fully unlocking robust black teas.
Patented Technology
Proprietary and exclusive to Chi Cha San Chen — the machine cannot be replicated, making quality consistency a built-in brand advantage.
Certified Quality
From Source to Cup
3-Tier Inspection
Quality is verified at the tea factory, at HQ via SGS testing, and at store level before every batch reaches the cup.
ISO 22000 & HACCP Certified
All raw materials carry ISO 22000 and HACCP international food safety certifications — from farm to finished drink.
International Taste Institute
All CCSC beverages have been recognised by the International Taste Institute (ITI) — an independent authority on taste excellence.
Direct Sourcing
Teas are sourced directly from high-mountain farms in Taiwan — traceability and terroir built into every blend.
