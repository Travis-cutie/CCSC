# Chi Cha San Chen — Brand Talking Points

---

## Obsessive Consistency: Every Cup, Identical

At Chi Cha San Chen, every cup of tea tastes exactly the same — not approximately, exactly. That's not an accident. It's the result of controlling every variable in the brewing process:

- **Water quality** is carefully managed, accounting for the difference between soft and hard water and how each affects flavor extraction.
- **Brew temperature** is precisely maintained for each tea type.
- **Tea leaves are leveled inside the teapresso machine** — a flat, even bed ensures every leaf is exposed to the same water flow, pressure, and brew time, eliminating inconsistency within a single serving.
- **All raw materials are measured with a margin of error of no more than 0.3mg** — every ingredient, including sugar, is controlled to this tolerance, eliminating human variation across the board.
- **Ice cubes are half-moon shaped**, engineered for a consistent melt rate so the drink doesn't dilute unevenly between first and last sip.
- **All equipment is recalibrated monthly** to ensure machines stay within spec — drift is caught before it reaches the cup.
- **Daily tea tastings** are conducted to catch any deviation in raw material quality before it reaches customers.

---

## Ingredient Integrity

- Tea is sourced from **high-altitude mountain farms** — grown at elevation where slower growth produces more complex flavor compounds. Because of the terrain, all leaves are **hand-harvested** using the 一心兩葉 (one bud, two leaves) method — only the youngest, most tender shoots are selected, ensuring peak flavor and quality in every pick.
- The brand's signature **green tea** is a standout proprietary blend, embodying the brand's dedication to sourcing rare, distinctive ingredients.
- **Green Tea** has been awarded the prestigious iTi 3-Star Superior Taste Award. Made from 100% fresh jasmine flowers and premium whole tea leaves, it undergoes a meticulous, multi-step scenting process to deliver a clean, crisp taste with a naturally sweet aftertaste and floral aroma.

---

## Teapresso Machine = Quality Control

The teapresso machine operates at a **precisely calibrated golden ratio** — the specific combination of time, temperature, and movement that produces Chi Cha San Chen's signature texture and clarity. This ratio is proprietary and central to the brand's replicability across locations.

---

## Award Recognition: ITI — The Michelin of Food

The **International Taste Institute (ITI)** is the global benchmark for food and beverage excellence — widely regarded as the Michelin Star equivalent for the food industry. Products are judged blind by a panel of international chefs and sommeliers.

Chi Cha San Chen has achieved **ITI Superior Taste Award — Three Stars (the highest level)** across all **6 of their base teas**. Their green tea has held the Three-Star rating for **seven consecutive years**, earning it the prestigious **ITI Diamond Certification** — the highest tier of recognition in the ITI system.
