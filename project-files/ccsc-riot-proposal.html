The Opportunity · 02

A fan engagement platform,
built around tea.

Riot's music universe has millions of dedicated fans who don't yet have a place to gather in real life. CCSC gives them one — a tea house that becomes a fan destination, one drink at a time.

Simple

A mechanic everyone understands

Buy a drink, get a collectible. No app required, no barrier to entry. Works from day one, scales without friction.

Fun

Collectibility drives return visits

Random pulls, rare variants, group-exclusive cards — the same mechanics that power K-pop and gaming fandom, applied to tea.

Measurable

Every purchase is trackable

QR codes, a digital passport, leaderboards — every interaction creates a data point, turning a gift-with-purchase into a fan-engagement system.

02
Core Mechanic · 03

Three steps. Endless loops.

The entire campaign is built on a mechanic so simple a first-time guest understands it in ten seconds — and deep enough to pull a dedicated fan back every week.

1

Buy the drink

Any CCSC × Riot signature drink — HEARTSTEEL, True Damage, or K/DA — at participating locations.

2

Get the collectible

One random photocard per drink. Group cards, member cards, holo rares, and location-exclusive variants. Chase the set. Trade with friends.

3

Join the experience

Scan the QR. Pick your artist. Earn points, climb the leaderboard, unlock digital rewards, and get notified for in-store events.

Beat 01 · Signature Drink · 05

HEARTSTEEL

Amber Black Tea
+ Passion Fruit
+ Lemon
+ Konjac

Bright, fast, playful, a little rebellious — the band's stage energy in a cup. The amber tea gives it depth; passion fruit and lemon bring the neon edge, while chewy konjac makes it feel collectible and different from a standard tea.

Electric & refreshing Punchy citrus finish Performance-ready
05
Beat 02 · Signature Drink · 06

TRUE DAMAGE

Amber Black Tea
+ Cream
+ Brown Sugar

Grounded, urban, music-video energy — heavier, grittier, more street than sweet. Brown sugar brings the raw, paint-drip edge; cream softens it just enough to feel premium and craveable.

Also pouring · Alternate

Amber Black Tea + Cream + Orange

Velvety and citrus-forward — like a grown-up orange creamsicle, with the depth of tea keeping it elevated and a little rebellious.

06
Beat 03 · Signature Drink · 07

K/DA

Amber Black Tea
+ Honey Milk
+ Taro Ball

Glamorous, refined, beautifully pastel. Honey milk gives the tea a silky sweetness; taro balls bring the soft purple cue that fits the K/DA world — light and indulgent without being heavy.

Suped up · Alternate

+ Honey Milk + Brown Sugar + Taro Ball

The main-stage version: creamy and dessert-like, finishing with warm, toffee-deep sweetness and comforting taro.

The Anchor · 24

The Digital Passport.

Not a full app — a mobile-first campaign microsite reached by QR on cards, sleeves, posters & receipts. It turns a gift-with-purchase into a trackable fan-engagement system: register a purchase, pick your artist, collect points & stamps, climb the leaderboard.

Phase 1

QR Campaign Hub

Scan, pick your artist, submit a code, enter weekly rewards.

Phase 2

Collection & Leaderboard

Digital stamps, points, live artist & store rankings.

Phase 3

Loyalty Foundation

Seasonal drinks, future collabs, events & long-term engagement.

Simple for stores. Fun for fans. Measurable for CCSC & Riot.

Simple to Run · Cleared to Ship · 25

Store kits & IP approval.

Pre-packed store kits

By week, store & drink type — signage, QR materials, code cards, photocards, reward inventory & a tracking sheet. Register flow stays: sell the drink → hand the gift → point to the QR. Premium rewards handled by managers/HQ.

IP approval tiers

CCSC
Tea visuals, store signage, generic graphics
Co-brand
CCSC × Riot lockups, key visuals, posters
Character IP
K/DA, HEARTSTEEL, True Damage art
Digital / Game
In-game rewards, QR unlocks, music

Establish the approval calendar before creative production — confirm which assets are launch-critical and which can wait for later phases.
